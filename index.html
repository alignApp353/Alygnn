FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you get started.

How does Alygnn match jobs?

Alygnn uses AI to read your resume and build a profile of your skills, experience, and seniority. We compare that profile against open roles and your stated criteria — location, salary floor, benefits — and surface opportunities designed to fit.

Is Alygnn free for job seekers?

Yes. Candidate accounts are free to create. You can upload your resume, build your profile, and preview matches without a credit card.

How quickly will I hear back after applying?

Response times depend on each employer. Alygnn is designed to improve the quality of matches so employers can review stronger-fit candidates faster.

Can I upload my resume privately?

Yes. Your resume and profile are used to generate your matches. You're always in control of what you share with employers.

What industries are supported?

Alygnn is built to support a wide range of industries, with an early focus on sales, business, technology, and professional services roles.
