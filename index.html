FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you get started.

How does Alygnn match jobs?

Alygnn uses AI to read your resume and build a profile of your skills, experience, and seniority. We compare that profile against open roles and your stated criteria — location, salary floor, benefits — and only surface jobs that genuinely fit.

Is Alygnn free for job seekers?

Yes, always. Candidate accounts are completely free — unlimited swipes, resume parsing, and application tracking, with no credit card required.

How do employers post jobs?

Employers sign up for an account, post a role through the employer dashboard, and start receiving AI-ranked candidates right away. Every plan includes a 15-day free trial.

Can I upload my resume privately?

Yes. Your resume and profile are only visible to employers once you apply to their role. You're always in control of what's shared and with whom.

What industries are supported?

Alygnn currently supports a wide range of industries, with a growing focus on tech, sales, business, and professional services roles. We're adding new categories regularly as we grow.
